We started the day with breakfast at the Best Western which was nearly as good as dinner the night before.

Then we proceeded up the road to the [stonehenge replica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maryhill_Stonehenge), and then to the [Mary Hill Museum](https://www.maryhillmuseum.org)

Next we traveled the [journey through time scenic byway](https://traveloregon.com/things-to-do/trip-ideas/scenic-drives/journey-through-time-scenic-byway/) to John Day, OR.

Where we stayed at the [Dreamers Lodge](https://www.dreamerslodge.com).

That night we shopped the entire town for a decent place to eat and settled upon [Outpost Pizza & Grill](https://www.outpostpizzapubgrill.com).
