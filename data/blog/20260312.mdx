First day I took the tram up it's 4,000ft vertical and the top was vicious with 70mph winds that stripped the snow right off the mountain and left nothing but ice!

There was a kid named Max who had a birthday party with him, we sang him happy birthday on the way up, the wind was blowing so hard when they exited the tram I thought they were gonna be blown right off the mountain.

Then during the day it started to snow hard and thunder...  THUNDERSNOW!!!

That night we got a decent drop and the next day was glorious, though I stayed toward the northern part of the mountain.
