Woke up today in Hatch and headed toward Bryce, along the way we had breakfast at the [Bryce Canyon Pines Cafe](https://bcpines.com/menu/).

Next hiked up the [Arches Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/arches-trail) in Red Canyon. 

Then we had lunch at [Ruby's Inn](https://www.rubysinn.com/restaurant-in-bryce-canyon/) at the [Cowboy's Buffet and Steak Room](https://www.rubysinn.com/restaurant-in-bryce-canyon/cowboys-buffet-steak-room/)

Then we hiked up to [Inspiration Point](https://www.nps.gov/brca/planyourvisit/inspiration.htm), which was `H0ly Sh!t!` Awesome!  I mean all mountain peaks have power, makes the hair on the back of my neck stand up everytime.  But `Inspiration Point` was worthy of it's title and then some, truly "Awe"-some.

Which is 8,320 ft, and the canyon floor is about 6600 ft or (1720 ft vertical)

This evening we stayed at the [Hyatt in Springdale](https://www.hyatt.com/hyatt-place/en-US/sguzz-hyatt-place-springdale-zion-national-park), and had dinner at the [Switchback Grill](https://www.switchbackgrille.com)

Starting with elk sliders, then an Incredible steak and amazing creamed spinach, along with a fantastic wedge salad.
