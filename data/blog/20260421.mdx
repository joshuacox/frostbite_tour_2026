Today we woke up in Cedar City, UT and had a fantastic breakfast at the [Best Western](https://www.bestwestern.com/en_US/book/hotels-in-cedar-city/best-western-plus-cedar-city/propertyCode.45091.html)

Then we traveled to [Kolob Canyon](https://www.nps.gov/zion/planyourvisit/kolob-canyons.htm) on the western side of Zion, 
that has many [trails](https://www.nps.gov/zion/planyourvisit/kolob-canyons-wilderness-hiking-trails.htm), 
we did the [Timber Creek Overlook](https://www.nps.gov/thingstodo/timber-creek-overlook.htm)

Which sits at about 6,250 ft with the creeks exiting the canyon at about 3,666 ft below. (2,584 ft vertical difference)

Here are some great [pics from Sheila Newenham](https://www.exploringnaturephotos.com/kolob-canyon-zion/)

Then we headed to [St. George, UT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._George,_Utah), [Virgin, UT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virgin,_Utah) where every citizen is mandated by law to own a firearm, and on through Zion to [Hatch, UT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hatch,_Utah) where we stayed for the night at the [Moutain Ridge Cabins](https://resnexus.com/resnexus/reservations/book/12551991-153D-4AD2-97A7-09A3DA18D152?googlehotels=11708&utm_source=google_hotel_ads&utm_medium=free_gha&GOOGLE-SITE=mapresults).
