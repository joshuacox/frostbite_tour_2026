For dinner had the fish and chips at [Anthonys](https://www.anthonys.com/restaurant/anthonys-homeport-des-moines/)

That night stayed in Puyallup, WA at the [Home Town Studios](https://www.redroof.com/checkout/select-rooms?DestId=HTS1049&adults=2&checkin=2026-04-27&checkout=2026-04-28&kids=0&rooms=1&useIATA=1&IATACode=TA003076&utm_medium=channel_connect&utm_campaign=google-hpa-4043&utm_source=google-hpa&src=goog_organic&utm_content=mapresults_27-04-2026_1_4043_US_desktop_default_unknown_unknown_organic&dsclid=77746389593104384), this was a funny little place with a little kithenette, but no cookware.  They would sell you a $50 set at the front desk if you were so inclined.  I ended up buying a [Blue diamon](https://bluediamondshopping.com) at a nearby walmart instead, and it is now one of my favorite pans.

The Home Town Studios was a funny little 'motel', that had signs about not staying more than 30 days, but then there was this one room that had a local preachers name on the door and a weird 'occupied' indicator on the door that he could set so that people would know he already had someone in there 'confessing' or whatever.
